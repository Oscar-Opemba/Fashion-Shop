Who we are

We are an online store built for shopping from a phone. Browse the catalogue, add what you want to your cart, and pay with M-Pesa without leaving the page.

How payment works

At checkout you enter the M-Pesa number you want to pay from. We send a payment request to that number, you enter your PIN on your handset, and the order is confirmed as soon as Safaricom tells us the payment went through. Nothing is charged until you enter your PIN.

Delivery

We deliver countrywide. Add your delivery details at checkout and we will be in touch once your order is on its way.
